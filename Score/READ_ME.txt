iannix.maxpat is an external object for Max/MSP written by Guillaume Jacquemin as part of the software Iannix (https://www.iannix.org/en/). The patch ‘Homoheliotropism_VoiceDisplay’ uses this object and requires it to be placed in your Max/MSP ‘externals’ or ‘packages’ folder.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

‘Homoheliotropism_VoiceDisplay’ is the three voices display for the performance of the Iannix score 'Homoheliotropisme’.
